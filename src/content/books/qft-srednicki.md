Srednicki's textbook takes a modern pedagogical approach to quantum field theory, organizing the material by spin rather than following the historical development. Starting with scalar fields and the path integral formalism, it builds up to spinor and gauge fields, covering renormalization, non-Abelian gauge theory, and aspects of the Standard Model in a clear and systematic way.
