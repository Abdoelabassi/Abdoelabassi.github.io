Steven Weinberg's magnum opus on quantum field theory begins from the foundations, deriving the necessity of quantum fields from the principles of Lorentz invariance and quantum mechanics. Volume 1 covers relativistic quantum mechanics, scattering theory, the cluster decomposition principle, and quantum electrodynamics with Weinberg's uniquely insightful perspective.
