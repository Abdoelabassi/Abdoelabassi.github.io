Yoshihiro Togashi crafts a masterpiece about ambition, friendship, and the cost of power. The series begins as a straightforward adventure but evolves into something far more complex, with incredible character development and world-building. From the Hunter Exam to the Dark Continent arc, each saga introduces deeper moral questions and more formidable challenges, making it one of manga's most celebrated works.
