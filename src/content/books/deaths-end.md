Liu Cixin's epic conclusion spans centuries and imagines civilizations at the edge of extinction. Death's End explores the implications of the dark forest theory to their ultimate conclusion, asking what it means to be human in a universe where survival requires becoming something you're not. A stunning finale that cements Liu Cixin as a master of speculative fiction.
