Liu Cixin's masterpiece combines hard science fiction with profound philosophical questions about survival, sacrifice, and the nature of civilization. The story begins during China's Cultural Revolution and expands into a cosmic-scale conflict between humanity and an advanced alien civilization struggling with the chaotic orbital mechanics of their three-sun system. A transformative work that redefined modern science fiction.
