The dark forest theory—that the universe is like a dark forest where every civilization is a hunter, and all are armed—becomes humanity's only defense. Liu Cixin deepens the scope of his saga, exploring the ethics of survival at any cost and the strategy of mutual assured destruction on a cosmic scale. This is science fiction at its most intellectually challenging and emotionally devastating.
