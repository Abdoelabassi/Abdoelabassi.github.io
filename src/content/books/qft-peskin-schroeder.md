The definitive introductory textbook on quantum field theory, widely used in graduate programs worldwide. Peskin and Schroeder provide a thorough treatment of quantum electrodynamics, renormalization group methods, non-Abelian gauge theories, and the Standard Model, with an emphasis on Feynman diagram calculations and physical intuition.
