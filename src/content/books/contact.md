Sagan's only novel weaves together rigorous science, philosophy, and adventure. Dr. Ellie Arroway discovers a signal from the star Vega and embarks on a journey that challenges humanity's understanding of the universe, consciousness, and belief. A profound meditation on science and faith, wrapped in a compelling narrative that only Sagan could write.
