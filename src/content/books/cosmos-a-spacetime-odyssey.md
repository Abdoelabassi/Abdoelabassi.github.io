Carl Sagan's magnum opus combines science, philosophy, and profound wonder. Cosmos takes readers on a guided tour of the universe, from the smallest atoms to the largest galaxies, while exploring the scientific method, the history of astronomy, and humanity's quest to understand our place in the cosmos. Essential reading for anyone curious about existence.
