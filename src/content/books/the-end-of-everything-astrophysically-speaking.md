Katie Mack makes the apocalyptic science of cosmic endings accessible and thrilling. She walks readers through five distinct scenarios of how the universe could ultimately end—from heat death to the Big Rip—all while explaining complex physics with remarkable clarity and humor. A perfect book for those fascinated by cosmology and the ultimate fate of existence.
