Chihiro Rokuhira, a young swordsmith's son, embarks on a quest for vengeance after his father is murdered and his enchanted blades are stolen. A fast-paced action manga blending swordsmanship with sorcery, Kagurabachi has quickly become one of the most popular new-generation Shonen Jump series with its stunning art and relentless pacing.
