A young ninja, Naruto Uzumaki, seeks recognition from his peers and dreams of becoming the Hokage, the leader of his village. An epic tale of perseverance, friendship, and the cycle of hatred in the shinobi world. Kishimoto masterfully weaves themes of loneliness, redemption, and the bonds that define us across 72 volumes of unforgettable storytelling.
