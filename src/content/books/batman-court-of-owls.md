Snyder introduces the Court of Owls, a shadowy organization that has controlled Gotham from the shadows, challenging Batman's knowledge of his own city. This arc is masterfully plotted, gorgeously illustrated by Capullo, and fundamentally reimagines Batman lore. The Talon assassins, the court's mystery, and Batman's psychological unraveling make this one of the greatest Batman stories ever told.
