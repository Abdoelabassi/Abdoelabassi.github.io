Bendis and Maleev's Daredevil is dark, intimate, and psychologically complex. This run strips away many of Daredevil's traditions and builds something fresh, exploring Matt Murdock's obsessive nature, his relationships, and the toll that being a hero takes on his life. Featuring iconic storylines like "Jinx" and "Parts of a Hole," this is essential reading for superhero fans.
